Expand description

Implements synchronized loading of *.png representation of tiles into grid_forge on the Rust side and [TileSet] on the Godot side.

Parallel loading is made to make sure that the tile_type_id of [BasicIdentTileData] matches between [VisCollection] and [GodotTileMapCollection].
